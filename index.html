At CCRAC we believe conversions should be simple and clear. As we further our purpose towards a better planet, we believe in making your life simple, articulate and impressively clear. That is why we came up with Conversions. With Conversions you can transfrom any of your coffee production statistics back and forth as needed in a timely and easily accessible. Oh and hey, we do have our own stats to convert so we do understand first hand behind the hustle of papers and calculators. Timely and accessible is our motto!

This is our base of operations in regards to coffee:

  • 7 kilograms of cherry makes one kilogram of parchment
  • 1 kilogram of parchment makes 0.8 kilograms of clean coffee
That is our criteria. For any enquiries as to the establishment of these statistical data please contact us here.
